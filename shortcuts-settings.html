Keyboard Shortcuts Settings

Customize keyboard shortcuts for highlighting text with different colors.

To customize keyboard shortcuts in Chrome:
1. Click the "Open Chrome Shortcuts" button below, or
2. Navigate to chrome://extensions/shortcuts
3. Find "Marks: Text Highlighter" and customize your shortcuts

To customize keyboard shortcuts in Firefox:
1. Navigate to about:addons
2. Click the gear icon and select "Manage Extension Shortcuts"
3. Find "Marks: Text Highlighter" and customize your shortcuts

Your Chrome version may not support programmatic shortcut updates. Please use chrome://extensions/shortcuts to customize shortcuts.
